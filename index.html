Core Beliefs

Salvation is a gift of grace, given through faith in the Most High. To enter the Kingdom of God, one must be born again—a spiritual rebirth, not a physical one, that transforms the heart and mind. This new life is found in surrender to His love, allowing the Spirit to guide us. Through repentance and faith, we turn away from our old ways and step into a life of purpose, peace, and true fulfillment as we are changed by His grace.

We believe that true faith requires taking responsibility for our lives, embracing gratitude each day, and acknowledging even the smallest blessings—food, water, a safe place to rest, family, friends, work, and the unique purpose we are called to fulfill. Living with such gratitude draws us closer to the Most High, and each day begins with a simple "thank you" for the gift of life and for the guidance of His Spirit.

To understand God fully, we turn to the Scriptures in their original form, studying the oldest texts and learning Hebrew to seek clarity and authenticity. This devotion to His Word grounds us, providing wisdom and light on our journey. We are careful about our physical health as well, choosing foods that nourish us—water rich with essential minerals, healthy foods, and an active lifestyle that strengthens us for the path ahead.

In this journey of faith, we stay focused on discovering our purpose and understanding the meaning of our lives. We look at our lives from all perspectives—recognizing where we’ve come from, where we are, and where we’re headed. As we grow, we ask ourselves: Are we surrounded by the right people? Are we building work and home environments that reflect peace, health, and spiritual strength? If we find that changes are needed, we make them with love and respect for the life we are given. We commit to living in a way that honors our purpose and draws us closer to the Most High, as we embrace the new life He has set before us.

The Guide of Faith

In today’s world, we’re surrounded by messages urging us to consume, accumulate, and endlessly chase after more. Yet deep within, we know that this path doesn’t lead to true fulfillment. Our core beliefs guide us, reminding us that a meaningful life is not built on endless consumption but on purpose, intention, and faith.

To live authentically, we break free from these hollow patterns. We make a conscious decision to reprogram our minds, to align our actions with the path that genuinely reflects our values. What you’re about to do here is more than an exercise or a checklist—it’s a plan for a life that embodies your beliefs, purpose, and inner vision.

Start by defining your core values—these are the unshakable truths that guide each step you take. Write them down as reminders of what truly matters. Let these values be your compass, keeping you aligned even as the world pulls you in different directions.

With these values in mind, envision the year ahead. Set a plan rooted in these beliefs: What goals resonate with your purpose? How do you wish to grow—spiritually, emotionally, and in your relationships with others? Outline what you hope to achieve in each area of your life over the coming months—whether it’s strengthening your faith, deepening connections, or fostering a healthier lifestyle.

Break these goals into smaller, manageable steps. Each step should feel like a purposeful choice, one that honors both your values and the vision you hold. Remember, these steps don’t have to be monumental; small, consistent actions are often the most transformative, leading you steadily toward a life that truly reflects who you are.

Let “The Guide of Faith” remind you: your journey isn’t about accumulating more but about choosing what truly adds value. Through intention, faith, and steady progress, you are creating a life that reflects your highest self, anchored in purpose and untouched by the world’s noise.

Seeking Truth

In our journey to understand the Universe, we dive deeply into sacred texts, not settling for easy answers but seeking truth with intention. We believe that true wisdom can be found through ancient writings, and we approach them with humility, researching, studying, and asking for guidance to ensure we are aligned with the most authentic sources. We ask the Universe to confirm its truth, trusting that clarity will come to those who earnestly seek.

Our actions are guided by a higher purpose, and we make it a daily practice to ask questions, to listen, and to seek understanding, knowing that every question has an answer waiting to be found. We are mindful in choosing teachers who share our values and our desire to seek truth. Yet, we don’t rely solely on their interpretations. We recognize that human understanding is limited, and only the wisdom of the Universe holds the ultimate authority.

Our path is one of discernment, listening to those who guide us while always returning to ancient wisdom, testing everything we learn against the core teachings of the Universe. We read, reflect, and seek clarity, grounding our beliefs in universal truth and letting it shape our lives. Each day, we commit to seeking, questioning, and allowing the wisdom of the Universe to lead us closer to truth, trusting that its light will illuminate the way.
